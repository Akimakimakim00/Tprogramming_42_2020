Subscriptions and their prices

Netflix offers a variety of plans to meet your needs. The plan you choose determines the number of devices you can watch Netflix on at the same time. With all our plans, you can download the Netflix app to all your favorite devices and watch unlimited movies and TV shows.

# subscription number of device price
1 basic 1 599р

Sign up for Netflix today and choose from several payment options. As a Netflix member, you pay once a month on the day you sign up. You have the freedom to change your plan or cancel it online at any time. You can get a free upgrade to a higher plan within the first month when you sign up or return. If you are on a plan with a free upgrade, we will ask if you want to keep the higher plan until your free upgrade ends.
